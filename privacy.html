Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wizard Clash ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Wizard Clash mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Wizard Clash does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising trackers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the game.

  • Anonymous account identifier generated through Firebase Authentication
  • Gameplay data such as Wizard, inventory, rewards, progression, PvP history, and tournament results
  • Technical information including device type, operating system, app version, and crash diagnostics
  • Purchase entitlement information required to validate in-app purchases

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Provide core gameplay functionality
  • Save and synchronize player progress
  • Deliver purchased content and rewards
  • Maintain game security and prevent abuse
  • Improve gameplay balance, stability, and performance

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play. Purchase validation and entitlement management may be handled using RevenueCat.

We do not collect or store payment card information, billing addresses, or financial credentials.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using Firebase infrastructure provided by Google. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

Information may be shared only with:

  • Infrastructure and service providers necessary to operate the app
  • Authorities or legal entities when required by applicable law

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly from inside the app through the "Delete Account" option.

Once an account deletion request is completed:

  • Gameplay data and associated account information are permanently removed
  • Access to purchases, Wizard, and progression is lost permanently
  • The deletion process cannot be reversed

8. Children's Privacy

Wizard Clash is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised date.
